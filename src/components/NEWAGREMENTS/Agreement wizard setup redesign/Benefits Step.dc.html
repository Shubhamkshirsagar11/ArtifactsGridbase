Benefits — behavior spec
Prototype annotation — not part of the product UI
BASIS DROPDOWN
Offers two presets — $ per hour worked and % of gross pay — plus Custom…, which opens a slide-over panel (separate frame). A custom basis shows as a summary chip, like the Pension row.
+ ADD FUND
Clicking "+ Add fund" first asks one question: "When does this fund's rate change? With the wage periods / On its own dates" — routing it to section 1 or section 2.
APPLIES TO
"One rate book…" fills that book's classes and marks all other books' classes Not applicable, in one act. "Specific classes…" opens a per-class editor (side panel).
SIMPLE AGREEMENTS
With one rate book in the agreement, the "One rate book…" option is hidden. Simple agreements see section 1 only, exactly like today.
