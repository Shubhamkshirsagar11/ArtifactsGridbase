Developer notes
Custom basis panel — behavior spec
Prototype annotation — not part of the product UI
Q1 → % OF PAY
Replaces questions 2–3 with: which wages it's a percent of (all vs worked-only hours · include benefit pay? · OT at straight-time?) plus an optional "plus $__ per hour worked" add-on.
Q1 → FLAT AMOUNT
Asks how often (day / week / month / pay run) and whether any hours or only worked hours trigger it.
Q2 → SINGLE HOUR TYPE
Picking "A single hour type" in question 2 hides question 3.
LIVE AGREEMENT DATA
"OT × 1.5 · DT × 2" in the premium option is this agreement's live data interpolated into the label — never hardcoded copy (California agreements read OT × 2.0).
DEVIATION INDICATORS
The "Starting from" bar and "modified" pills show how this custom config deviates from the preset the user began with.
